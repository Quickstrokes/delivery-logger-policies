Privacy Policy

DeliveryLogger Mobile Application

Last Updated: September 3, 2025

Home / Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

DeliveryLogger ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name and email address from your social login provider (Google, Facebook, or Microsoft)
  • Delivery Data: Delivery records, addresses, customer information, and payment details you enter
  • Restaurant Information: Restaurant name and POS system configuration (for restaurant owners)

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: App features used, frequency of use, and performance metrics
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the DeliveryLogger service
  • Authenticate your identity and manage your account
  • Sync delivery data between restaurant owners and drivers
  • Import orders from POS systems (with your authorization)
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using:

  • Microsoft Azure: Cloud storage with enterprise-grade security
  • Azure Key Vault: Encrypted storage for sensitive credentials
  • SSL/TLS Encryption: All data transmissions are encrypted
  • Authentication: Secure authentication via Microsoft Azure AD B2C

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share data only:

  • Between restaurant owners and their authorized drivers within the app
  • With POS systems you explicitly authorize for order imports
  • When required by law or to protect our legal rights
  • With service providers who assist in app operations (under strict confidentiality)

6. Third-Party Services

We integrate with the following third-party services:

  • Authentication Providers: Google, Facebook, Microsoft
  • POS Systems: Toast, Aldelo, Square, Clover (with your permission)
  • Cloud Services: Microsoft Azure

Each service has its own privacy policy that governs their use of your information.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your delivery records
  • Opt-out of non-essential data collection
  • Revoke POS system access at any time

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion:

  • Personal data is deleted within 30 days
  • Anonymized analytics data may be retained for improvement purposes
  • Data required for legal compliance may be retained as necessary

9. Children's Privacy

DeliveryLogger is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email.

11. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or concerns:

  • Email: privacy@deliverylogger.com
  • Account Deletion: Request Account Deletion

12. Compliance

This privacy policy complies with:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Apple App Store Guidelines
  • Google Play Store Policies
