⚠️ Important Information

Deleting your account will permanently remove all your data, including:

  • Your profile information
  • All delivery records and history
  • Restaurant configurations (for owners)
  • Driver assignments (for owners)
  • Access to any connected POS systems

This action cannot be undone.

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your DeliveryLogger account using any of these methods:

Method 1: Through the App (Recommended)

  1. Open the DeliveryLogger app on your device
  2. Navigate to Settings (gear icon)
  3. Scroll to Account Management
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm your identity
  6. Review the deletion warning
  7. Type "DELETE" to confirm
  8. Tap Permanently Delete Account

Method 2: Email Request

Send an email to support@deliverylogger.com with:

  • Subject: "Account Deletion Request"
  • The email address associated with your account
  • Your full name as it appears in the app
  • Reason for deletion (optional)

We will verify your identity and process the request within 48 hours.

Method 3: Web Form

Note: This form sends an email request. You will receive a confirmation email to verify your identity.

What Happens Next?

Immediately:

Your account is deactivated and you're logged out of all devices

Within 24 hours:

Your data is removed from active systems

Within 30 days:

All personal data is permanently deleted from backups

After 30 days:

Complete deletion confirmed via email

Data Export

Before deleting your account, you may want to export your data:

  • In the app, go to Settings → Export Data
  • Choose format (CSV or JSON)
  • Select date range
  • Tap "Export" to download your data

Reactivation

Once your account is deleted:

  • You cannot reactivate it or recover any data
  • You can create a new account with the same email after 90 days
  • Your username becomes available for others after deletion

Contact Support

If you have questions about account deletion or need assistance:

  • Email: support@deliverylogger.com
  • Response time: Within 48 hours

Legal Compliance
